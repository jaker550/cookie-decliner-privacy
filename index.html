Introduction

Auto Decline Cookies ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension operates and what data (if any) is collected when you use it.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The Extension:

  • Does NOT collect your browsing history
  • Does NOT track which websites you visit
  • Does NOT store any personal information
  • Does NOT send any data to external servers
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does NOT create user profiles

How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely locally in your browser:

  1. Cookie Banner Detection: The Extension scans web pages you visit for cookie consent banners
  2. Automatic Clicking: When a banner is detected, it automatically clicks "Reject All" or similar buttons
  3. Local Operation: All processing happens locally in your browser - nothing is sent externally

Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following browser permissions:

storage: Used to store extension settings locally on your device (no data is sent externally)

activeTab: Allows the Extension to interact with the current webpage to detect and click cookie banners

all_urls: Required to run the Extension on all websites you visit to automatically decline cookies

These permissions are used solely for the Extension's core functionality and no data is collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The Extension includes a donation link to Buy Me a Coffee (buymeacoffee.com/nhftools). Clicking this link opens an external website. Please refer to Buy Me a Coffee's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Browser Storage

The Extension may store minimal preference data locally in your browser using the browser's storage API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted at any time by removing the Extension
