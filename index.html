Privacy Policy for Auto Decline Cookies

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

Auto Decline Cookies ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Collection

What We Collect

Payment Information:

  • When you subscribe, payment processing is handled by ExtensionPay (our payment processor) and Stripe
  • We store only: subscription status (paid/trial/expired) and trial start date
  • We do NOT store credit card details, billing addresses, or payment information

Usage Data:

  • Installation date
  • Extension on/off status
  • NO browsing history
  • NO personal information
  • NO website content
  • NO tracking cookies

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • ❌ Website content you visit
  • ❌ Search queries
  • ❌ Cookies or tracking data
  • ❌ Any data from websites you visit

How We Use Data

The extension uses the collected data ONLY to:

  1. Determine if you have an active subscription
  2. Track trial period expiration
  3. Function properly (detect and click cookie banners)
Important: The extension works entirely locally in your browser. It does not send your browsing data to any server.

Data Storage

  • Subscription data is stored in Chrome's sync storage (syncs across your devices)
  • No data is stored on external servers except:
    • Payment status (stored by ExtensionPay for subscription management)

Third-Party Services

ExtensionPay

  • Handles subscription management and payments
  • Privacy Policy: https://extensionpay.com/privacy
  • They process: subscription status and payment information via Stripe

Stripe

  • Processes payments securely
  • Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy
  • We never see your payment details

Permissions Explained

The extension requests these permissions:

  • "storage" - To save your subscription status locally
  • "activeTab" - To detect and click cookie banners on the current tab
  • "notifications" - To show subscription confirmation (optional)
  • "Access your data for all websites" - Required to detect cookie banners on any website you visit. The extension ONLY looks for cookie banner buttons and does NOT collect, store, or transmit your browsing data.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with third parties (except payment processor)
  • Track your browsing
  • Collect analytics
  • Use advertising networks

We ONLY share:

  • Subscription status with ExtensionPay (necessary for payment processing)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (subscription status) at any time through the extension popup
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through ExtensionPay
  • Request information about what data we have (contact us)

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Data Retention

  • Subscription data: Kept while you're subscribed, deleted 30 days after unsubscription
  • Local data: Deleted immediately when you uninstall the extension

This extension helps you decline cookies automatically. We don't collect your browsing data, track you, or sell information. We only know if you've paid for the extension. The extension works locally in your browser and doesn't send your data anywhere.
